Welcome to Al's Auto

Your trusted partner for quality car care and maintenance.

Services

We handle everything your vehicle needs, from routine upkeep to complex fixes but we focus our experience where it matters most. Below are the areas we specialise in, each one reflects the careful diagnosis, quality parts, and skilled workmanship we bring to every job.

Minor Service

oil and filter changes, fluid top-ups, brake checks, tyre pressure and basic safety checks to keep your car running reliably between major services.

Major Service

comprehensive scheduled maintenance including timing checks, belt and hose inspections, spark plugs, full fluid replacements, and detailed systems testing to extend the life of your vehicle.

Vehicle Inspection

thorough pre-purchase, roadworthy, or safety inspections with written reports and honest recommendations so you know exactly what condition the car is in.

Electrical Repair

diagnosis and repair of batteries, alternators, starters, wiring faults, sensors, and electronic modules that keep modern cars functioning correctly.

Mechanical Repair

engine, transmission, suspension, steering, and brake repairs performed with precision to restore performance and safety.

About Us

What began as a family hobby quickly became our livelihood. I loved taking engines apart and putting them right again, and neighbors started asking for help — one job led to another, and today we run a full-time, family-owned auto repair shop. We combine years of hands-on experience with up-to-date techniques to diagnose and repair everything from routine maintenance to complex mechanical issues.

Our approach is simple: do the job properly, explain it in plain language, and stand behind our work. That means honest pricing, timely turnarounds, and a level of care you don’t get from large chains. Whether it’s a scheduled service or an unexpected breakdown, our goal is to get you back on the road safely and confidently.
